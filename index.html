The Ultimate Guide to Authenticating LABUBU Collectibles

Are you a fan of LABUBU, the mischievous, toothy monster created by artist Kasing Lung? As its popularity soars, so does the prevalence of counterfeits. This guide is your essential resource for verifying your collection and ensuring your next purchase is authentic. Don't get scammed – learn how to check Labubu and differentiate real from fake.

Key Authentication Principles:

Authenticating your LABUBU involves a meticulous check of several crucial aspects. Pay close attention to the details below:

1. Appearance & Feel (Plush Texture & Facial Details)

The initial touch and visual inspection can reveal a lot about a LABUBU's authenticity.

Plush Texture:
  • Authentic: Feels like a high-end plush toy—soft, smooth, and dense, with a rich, vibrant color.
  • Fake: Feels cheap and coarse, with a faded color and sparse, uneven fur.

Side-by-side comparison of authentic vs. fake plush texture.

Facial Details:
  • Authentic: Features a natural blush gradient, glossy and well-set eyes with distinct white highlights. The iconic "toothy grin" should be well-defined and consistently painted.
  • Fake: Often exhibits harsh, dull colors, eyes that lack a clear highlight, or incorrect eye shape. The grin might be poorly rendered or misaligned.

Differences in eye gloss, blush, and grin precision.

2. Key Details (Foot Logo & Wash Tag)

Small markings can often betray a counterfeit. Don't overlook these subtle yet critical indicators.

Foot Logo:
  • Authentic: Typically features "Pop Mart" on the left foot and "Kasing Lung" on the right. The print is clear, smooth, and precise, without smudges.
  • Fake: The print is often blurry, rough, or may even have spelling errors or incorrect brand/artist names.

Close-up comparison of foot logos: clarity vs. blurriness/errors.

Wash Tag:
  • Authentic: Stitched flat, feels smooth, with clear, dark black text. Information such as material composition and care instructions should be accurate and legible.
  • Fake: Can feel more like cheap plastic, with a raised texture and faded gray text. Information might be incomplete, incorrect, or contain grammatical errors.

Comparison of text quality and material feel of wash tags.

3. Packaging & Accessories (Box Quality, Security Label, Inner Bag, Desiccant Packet)

The packaging itself is a significant indicator of authenticity, as counterfeiters often cut corners here.

Box Quality & Color:
  • Authentic: Features a matte finish box with vibrant, accurate colors and sharp printing of designs and text. The box material feels sturdy and premium.
  • Fake: Often glossy, with washed-out or incorrect colors and blurry text. The box material might feel flimsy.

Side-by-side box comparisons: color vibrancy, print sharpness, material quality.

Security Label:
  • Authentic: Features a holographic label on the outside of the box with a clear, flashing 'P' logo (for Pop Mart) or other specific security features. This label should be well-adhered and intact.
  • Fake: Usually lacks this label, or the logo is unclear, poorly printed, or easily peels off.

Authentic holographic security label vs. missing/poorly made fake one.

Inner Bag:
  • Authentic: Has a clear sealing edge, air holes for breathability, and a mirrored logo inside (if applicable to the series). The material feels durable.
  • Fake: Lacks these features, the sealing edge might be rough, or the material feels thin and brittle.

Comparison of inner bags: sealing, air holes, and logo presence.

Desiccant Packet:
  • Authentic: The desiccant packet is printed with a clear POP MART logo or other official branding. It should appear professionally manufactured.
  • Fake: The desiccant packet has no branding, generic printing, or appears cheaply made.

Comparison of desiccant packets: branding and quality.

Advanced "Scam-Proof" Tips:

Be aware of sophisticated scams that try to trick even experienced collectors.

The "Authentic Box, Fake Doll" Scam:

A successful QR code scan does NOT guarantee the doll is authentic! Scammers use a real box with a valid QR code, but a cheap counterfeit is placed inside the real box. The user scans the code, it passes, but the doll is a fake.

Key Takeaway:

  • You MUST physically inspect the doll itself.
  • The official verification URL is usually twzy.popmart.com or similar.
  • A "first-time query" result after scanning the QR code is a better sign.
The UV Light (Blacklight) Trick:

This is a simple and effective advanced technique, but be aware that reactions vary by series.

  • Have A Seat (V2) Series: This series has the clearest UV security feature. Under a blacklight, the bottom of the right foot of an authentic doll will show a clear silhouette matching the character's pose, glowing fluorescent blue. The eyes, mouth, and nose may also react.
  • Exciting Macaron (V1) Series: Authentic dolls from this series do not have a UV mark on the foot. However, some collectors note that the teeth will show a clear fluorescent reaction under a blacklight.
  • General Tips: For many of the plush keychain series, a common trick is to shine the UV light on the nose, as the paint used on authentic dolls often glows. Note that UV features on series like "Big Into Energy" (V3) are inconsistent and should not be the only basis for authentication.

Final Thoughts:

By diligently following these authentication guidelines, you can confidently check Labubu items and safeguard your valuable collection. Always source your LABUBU from reputable sellers and when in doubt, cross-reference with official guides and trusted communities. Your peace of mind, and the authenticity of your collection, is paramount.

The Monsters Series Details

The Monsters series is the foundational collection that first introduced the beloved Labubu character and its whimsical companions to the world. This series is characterized by its playful and mischievous monster designs, each with unique features and expressions. It captures the essence of Kasing Lung's artistic vision, combining cute aesthetics with a touch of eccentricity. Collectors often start their journey with this iconic series due to its classic appeal and the variety of charming figures it offers. The designs typically feature Labubu in various monster forms, often with its signature toothy grin and expressive eyes, alongside other characters like Zimomo, Spooky, and Pato.

Sports Series Details

The Sports series showcases Labubu and friends in various athletic endeavors, bringing a dynamic and energetic flair to the collectible line. This series features characters dressed in sports attire, equipped with relevant gear, and often posed in action-oriented stances. From basketball to swimming, and even lesser-known sports, the series captures the spirit of competition and teamwork. It's a popular choice for collectors who appreciate an active theme or are sports enthusiasts themselves. The figures often incorporate clever details related to their sport, making them unique additions to any collection.

Zimomo Crossover Details

The Zimomo Crossover series is a special collaboration that merges Labubu with another iconic character from Kasing Lung's creative universe: Zimomo. This series explores the playful interaction and stylistic blend between these two distinct figures. Collectors can expect to see Labubu adorned with elements or characteristics of Zimomo, or vice versa, creating unique and highly sought-after designs. It's a testament to the versatility of Labubu's design and the expansive world Kasing Lung has built, offering fans a fresh perspective on their favorite characters. These crossover figures often represent significant releases within the Labubu collection.

The Farmer Series Details

The Farmer series invites Labubu into a charming rustic setting, portraying the mischievous character and its friends as various farm animals and workers. This collection embodies a quaint and heartwarming theme, with figures dressed in overalls, holding farming tools, or resembling barnyard creatures. The designs often feature earthy tones and wholesome aesthetics, offering a delightful contrast to some of Labubu's more whimsical or urban themes. It's a series that appeals to collectors looking for figures with a pastoral feel and a touch of everyday charm, showcasing Labubu's adaptability to different conceptual roles.

Exciting Macaron Series Details

The Exciting Macaron series is a truly delightful collection that infuses Labubu with the vibrant and sweet aesthetics of macarons. Each figure in this series is adorned with colorful, dessert-inspired accessories and features, often mimicking the delightful pastel hues and intricate textures of the popular French confectionery. This series is known for its cheerful and eye-catching designs, making it a favorite among collectors who appreciate cute and vibrant themes. The characters might be seen holding macarons, wearing macaron-shaped hats, or simply embodying the sweet charm of these treats, adding a delicious touch to any collection.

Flower Elves Series Details

The Flower Elves series transports Labubu and its companions into an enchanting natural world, transforming them into charming flower-inspired elves. This collection celebrates the beauty of flora, with each figure intricately designed to incorporate elements of various flowers and plants. From delicate petals to leafy adornments, the designs are whimsical and evoke a sense of fantasy and magic. This series appeals to collectors who appreciate nature-themed aesthetics and imaginative character transformations. The figures often feature soft color palettes and elegant details, making them a beautiful and serene addition to any Labubu display.

Where to buy Labubu

Finding authentic Labubu collectibles can be a thrilling part of the collecting journey. To ensure you're getting genuine products and supporting official channels, consider the following reputable sources:

Official Pop Mart Stores & Websites:

The safest bet is always to purchase directly from Pop Mart's official channels. This includes their physical retail stores located in various countries and their official online web stores. Official online stores are typically region-specific, so ensure you are on the correct one for your location (e.g., Pop Mart Global, Pop Mart China, Pop Mart SEA).

Always look for secure payment gateways and clear return policies.

Visit Pop Mart Global Official Site

Authorized Retailers:

Many licensed toy stores and collectible shops are authorized to sell Pop Mart products. These retailers often receive their stock directly from Pop Mart or official distributors. Examples include:

  • Large department stores with toy sections (e.g., in Asia, select malls globally)
  • Specialty collectible stores (e.g., blind box shops, designer toy stores)
  • Reputable online hobby shops

It's always a good idea to check if a retailer is listed as an official partner on Pop Mart's website, if such a list is available.

Reputable Online Marketplaces (with caution):

While third-party marketplaces can offer a wider selection, exercise caution. Look for sellers with:

  • High ratings and a long history of positive reviews.
  • Clear photos of the actual product (not stock photos).
  • Detailed descriptions including box condition, and authentication features.
  • Fair pricing that isn't suspiciously low.

Platforms like eBay, Etsy, or local equivalents can be good if you vet sellers thoroughly. Avoid unverified sellers on social media or unknown websites.

Collector Communities and Forums:

Joining online collector communities (e.g., on Reddit, Facebook groups, specialized forums) can connect you with trusted sellers and facilitate trades. These communities often have strict rules against scams and members can vouch for each other's authenticity. Always ensure transactions are done through secure methods.

Remember: If a deal seems too good to be true, it probably is. Prioritize authenticity over price to protect your collection.
