Authenticity in Your Hands.

Labubu, the mischievous, toothy monster created by artist Kasing Lung, has become a global pop culture icon. As its popularity grows, so does the challenge of finding authentic items. This guide is your essential resource for verifying your collection and purchasing with confidence.

The Ultimate Authentication Guide

Master authentication techniques, from the big picture to the finest details.

Plush Texture

Authentic: Feels like a high-end plush toyâ€”soft, smooth, and dense, with a rich color.

Fake: Feels cheap and coarse, with a faded color and sparse, uneven fur.

Facial Details

Authentic: Natural blush gradient, glossy and well-set eyes with distinct white highlights.

Fake: Harsh blush, dull eyes that lack a clear highlight, or incorrect eye shape.

Foot Logo

Authentic: "Pop Mart" on the left foot, "Kasing Lung" on the right. The font is clear and smooth.

Fake: The print is often blurry, rough, or may even have spelling errors.

Wash Tag

Authentic: Stitched flat, feels smooth, with clear, dark black text.

Fake: Can feel more like plastic, with a raised texture and faded gray text.

Box Quality & Color

Authentic: Matte finish box with vibrant, accurate colors and sharp printing.

Fake: Often glossy, with washed-out or incorrect colors and blurry text.

Security Label

Authentic: Features a holographic label on the outside of the box with a clear, flashing double 'P' logo.

Fake: Usually lacks this label, or the logo is unclear.

Inner Bag

Authentic: Has a clear sealing edge, air holes, and a mirrored logo inside.

Fake: Lacks these features.

Desiccant Packet

Authentic: The desiccant packet is printed with a clear POP MART logo.

Fake: The desiccant packet has no branding.
